Final Countdown for the Brooklyn Hurricanes

As concerns about head injuries continue, tackle football players turn to flag football.

By Mallika Mitra

After 60 years, the legacy of the Brooklyn Hurricanes has come to an end. The youth tackle football league has seen a drop in registration due to concerns around head injuries.
